Travel Information

Check-in and Arrival

Can I change my reservations online after I check in?

No. You can not change your reservation online once you have checked in. Contact reservations@alas.com for assistance. 

What if i have checked in, but I am unable to make my flight?

If you’re unable to make your flights, contact Reservations prior to your flight’s departure to retain the value of your ticket. 

When do I need to provide my U.S. Residents Card?

When you’ve indicated the United States as your country of residence and your passport nationality is other than the United States, you will be asked to provide U.S. Resident Card information.

What form of payment can I use?

Seats

Go to "My Trips" under your account and choose your desired seats. If seats are unavailable, check back later or change your seats when you check-in.

Receipt and Refunds

You have 24 hours to refund your ticket if you purchased the tickets at least 4 days prior to departure and you must cancel the ticket to receive a refund.
You cannot receive a refund on nonrefundable tickets unless you cancel it 24 hours after purchasing it. You may receive a refund on pet fees or bag fees 
if you were unable to use them. You can receive a receipt on most tickets booked in the past 10 months, just enter a 13 digit ticket number and the 
passenger name.

Conditions of Carriage

We here at Alas Airlines connect people, take them to places and give them memories to look back on. In order to provide our passengers with a safe, 
friendly environment and to make everything run smoothly, you are expected to meet and or be aware of these policies. 

You as a passenger are expected to:
  • Provide a valid photo accepted by TSA, Visa, any other traveling documents
  • Allow your bags to be inspected by TSA, customs, or any other government officials
  • Comply with all safety instructions
  • Postpone your flight if you have a dangerous disease that could be spread to others
Children boarding
  • Infants under 2 years old can be held in your lap for free, but if you have a seat for them you are required to pay a fee.
  • Infants under 1 month must have permission by a doctor to board a plane. 
  • Children under 6 years old cannot travel alone.
Delays and Preperation 
  • If any event that is put of our control (Acts of God, Civil disturbances, Acts of terror, ect.) occurs we provide for a refund on the majority of fees.
  • If a delay occurs, we can provide comfort as you wait. If we are unable to board within 30 minutes of delay, we can provide a place for passengers to stay (hotel, private area in airport.)
  • Be ready to arrive at the gate 10 mins before domestic flight and 30 mins before for international flights.
